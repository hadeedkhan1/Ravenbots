ABOUT ICBC FTC ROBOTICS

ICBC Robotics is a team of high school students who are passionate about robotics and engineering. We compete in the FIRST Tech Challenge, a robotics competition that challenges students to design, build, program, and operate robots to compete in a head-to-head challenge in an alliance format.
