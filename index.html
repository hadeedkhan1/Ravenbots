About Us

ICBC Ravenbots is a team of high school students who are passionate about robotics and engineering. We compete in the FIRST Tech Challenge, a robotics competition that challenges students to design, build, program, and operate robots to compete in a head-to-head challenge in an alliance format.

Goals & Perspectives

FLL Teams

Our robotics team proudly has several FLL teams, fostering innovation and teamwork. We provide mentorship, resources, and a supportive environment to help young minds excel in robotics competitions. By working alongside FLL teams, we inspire creativity and problem-solving in the next generation of engineers.

Community Engagement

We believe robotics should be accessible to everyone, which is why we actively engage with our community. Through workshops, demonstrations, and outreach programs, we spark curiosity and excitement about STEM. By connecting with schools and local organizations, we empower individuals to explore the world of robotics.

Technology Skills

Our mission is to equip young minds with essential technology skills for the future. We provide hands-on experience in coding, electronics, and engineering, helping students develop critical thinking and innovation. By fostering a passion for technology early on, we prepare the next generation to lead in an ever-evolving digital world.
